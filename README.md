Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption, market trends, and performance metrics (like range and make/model popularity). Provides key insights for strategic planning in the e-mobility sector.

#  ‚ú® Key Features & Insights

This dashboard transforms raw EV data into actionable insights, enabling the analysis of the following key areas:

Geographic Adoption: Visualize the distribution and concentration of EVs (BEV/PHEV) across regions, states, or counties to identify adoption hotspots.

Manufacturer & Model Trends: Track the market share, sales growth, and overall vehicle count for top EV manufacturers and popular models over time.

Range & Performance Metrics: Analyze the relationship between a vehicle's electric range, base MSRP, and battery type (BEV vs. PHEV).

Time Series Analysis: Examine the year-over-year (YoY) and month-over-month (MoM) growth in EV registrations to understand market acceleration.
